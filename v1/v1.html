02 / Foundations

Typography

Primary is Inter for clean body rhythm, Space Grotesk for expressive headlines and Geist Mono for precision metadata. All three are loaded through the same asset strategy already present in the referenced templates.

Font loading strategy

Primary: `Inter`, sans-serif

Display: `Space Grotesk`, `Inter`, sans-serif

Monospace: `Geist Mono`, `SFMono-Regular`, monospace

Loading: local CSS assets linked from referenced templates, weight ranges reused from those files, no new external dependency introduced.

Usage intent

Display roles use transparent or ultra-tight lettering for premium sections and hero moments.

Body roles stay neutral and highly legible for specs, descriptions and long-form guidelines.

Mono roles are reserved for measurements, metadata, labels and token naming.

Opacity variants & gradients

`rgba(255,255,255,0.82)` is the strong glass panel. `rgba(255,255,255,0.5-0.68)` is the default frosted layer.

`rgba(23,23,23,0.14)` and `0.24` drive border hierarchy and separator strength.

Gradients participate mainly as atmospheric light: white-to-transparent, champagne-to-transparent, and occasional ink-to-transparent overlays over imagery.

Contextual usage rules
Text: ink primary, stone secondary, mono for labels, white only on dark buttons or image overlays.
Background: neutral white base, blurred white panels, warm accent only as tint or glow.
Border: muted low-opacity neutral, strengthen on hover or focus, never fully black on the white canvas.
Hover / Active / Disabled: lift + glow / inverted ink or accent / desaturated secondary with reduced contrast.
